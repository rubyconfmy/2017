Code of Conduct

The Ruby community has a strong legacy of being respectful and nice (because MINASWAN). As part of this global community and responsible individual in general, we expect all attendees to respect each other, and themselves, during all events organized by RubyConf MY.

The RubyConf MY team pledges to provide a safe, welcoming and harassment-free environment for all participants. By participating in our events, all attendees (audience, speakers, sponsors, organizing partners, staff, volunteers, etc) must agree and adhere to the conference Code of Conduct at all times.

We stand strongly against any and all sorts of abusive and harassing behaviour, be it physical, verbal or emotional. We will not hesitate to take action, legal or otherwise, against anyone found guilty of such act.

We expect full cooperation from everybody participanting in our events. Do not hesitate to contact us at abuse@rubyconf.my or get in touch with our ground staffs should you experience or witness any harassments over the course of our events.

Please read and understand the Code of Conduct before continuing further.
